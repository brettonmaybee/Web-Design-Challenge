Per Capita Income vs Other Social Factors

The purpose of this project was to analyze the relationship between per capita income other social data collected from 182 countries in 2019. The data was collected from UN sources found at www.gapminder.org/data/. After assembling the data with Excel, I used Matplotib to plot per capita income against other social data from 2019. These factors included: birth rate, infant mortality, life expectancy, and C02 consumption. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, as well as descriptions of the trends and correlations witnessed.
