Income vs Birth Rate

This plot shows the relationship between per capita income, and the average amount of children born to each mother. This plot shows that countries with a lower per capita annual income, tend to have a much higher birthrate than countries with a higher per capita income. This may be the result of higher infant mortality, and a lack of family planning services in poorer nations. The UN data used for this plot can be found at www.gapminder.org/data.
